Neon Genesis Evangelion
Neon Genesis Evangelion is a Japanese mecha anime series produced by Gainax and Tatsunoko Production, directed by Hideaki Anno and broadcast on TV Tokyo from October 1995 to March 1996. The story follows the teenage boy Shinji Ikari, who is recruited by his father Gendo Ikari to pilot the giant mecha called "Evangelions" to protect the world from mysterious creatures known as Angels. The series has spawned numerous spin-offs, including manga, video games, and films.
The Evangelions are giant cyborgs created by NERV, a secret organization under the United Nations. They are piloted by fourteen-year-old children chosen for their special abilities.
